Pollution is when harmful things like trash, chemicals, or smoke get into our air, water, and soil. This can make it hard for plants, animals, and even people to live and grow. For example, dirty air can make it hard for us to breathe, and polluted water can make fish and other animals sick. By keeping our environment clean, we help all living things stay healthy and happy!

You can help the planet by following these rules:

  1. Reduce, Reuse, Recycle: Try to use less plastic and recycle items like paper, glass, and metal.
  2. Save Energy: Turn off lights and electronics when you’re not using them to save electricity.
  3. Plant Trees: Trees help clean the air by taking in carbon dioxide and giving out oxygen.
  4. Use Eco-friendly Products: Choose products that are safe for the environment, like reusable bags and water bottles.
  5. Don’t Litter: Always throw trash in the bin and help clean up litter when you see it.
